Mars in Leo reflects a confident, expressive, and pride-driven approach to action.
Energy is directed toward self-expression, creativity, and personal recognition.

This placement values leadership, courage, and dramatic engagement.
Motivation is fueled by passion and the desire to be seen or appreciated.

While enthusiasm is strong, sensitivity to criticism may trigger defensiveness.
Mars in Leo acts best when creativity and confidence are honored.
