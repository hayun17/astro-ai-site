Mars in Sagittarius reflects an adventurous, optimistic, and freedom-seeking approach to action.
Energy is directed toward exploration, learning, and expansion.

This placement values honesty, movement, and personal growth.
Motivation arises through purpose, belief, or future-oriented goals.

While enthusiasm is strong, follow-through may suffer without clear direction.
Mars in Sagittarius acts best when guided by meaningful vision.
