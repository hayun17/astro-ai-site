Mars in Cancer indicates an emotionally driven and protective approach to action.
Energy is influenced by mood, intuition, and personal attachment.

This placement values emotional security and the protection of loved ones.
Motivation often arises from the need to defend or nurture.

While emotional depth is a strength, indirect expression of anger may occur.
Mars in Cancer acts best when emotional needs are acknowledged and respected.
