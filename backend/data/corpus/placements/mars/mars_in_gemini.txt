Mars in Gemini reflects a mentally active, curious, and versatile approach to action.
Energy is expressed through communication, learning, and the exchange of ideas.

This placement values adaptability, quick thinking, and intellectual stimulation.
Motivation often shifts between interests, favoring variety over sustained focus.

While mental agility is strong, scattered energy may reduce follow-through.
Mars in Gemini acts best when mentally engaged and challenged.
