Venus in Leo reflects a warm, expressive, and wholehearted approach to love.
Affection is shown openly, often with generosity, creativity, and dramatic flair.

This placement values appreciation, admiration, and emotional sincerity.
Romance is experienced as a source of joy and self-expression.

While devotion is strong, there may be sensitivity to feeling unappreciated.
Venus in Leo seeks love that feels meaningful, passionate, and celebrated.
