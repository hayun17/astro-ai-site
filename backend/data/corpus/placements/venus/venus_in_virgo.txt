Venus in Virgo indicates a thoughtful, attentive, and service-oriented approach to love.
Affection is expressed through practical support, reliability, and careful consideration.

This placement values sincerity, usefulness, and emotional clarity.
Love is often demonstrated through actions rather than overt emotional displays.

While devotion is strong, self-criticism or high standards may limit emotional ease.
Venus in Virgo seeks love that is grounded, meaningful, and mutually supportive.
