Venus in Gemini reflects a curious, playful, and mentally oriented approach to relationships.
Attraction is sparked through conversation, shared ideas, and intellectual stimulation.

This placement values variety, communication, and emotional lightness.
Connection is maintained through dialogue and mutual curiosity rather than emotional intensity alone.

While charm and adaptability are strengths, consistency may be challenged by shifting interests.
Venus in Gemini seeks love that feels engaging, flexible, and mentally alive.
