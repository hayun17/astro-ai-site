Venus in Capricorn indicates a reserved, committed, and goal-oriented approach to love.
Affection is expressed through responsibility, consistency, and long-term dedication.

This placement values reliability, maturity, and shared ambitions.
Emotional expression may be measured, but loyalty is taken seriously.

While emotional warmth may develop slowly, commitment is enduring.
Venus in Capricorn seeks love that can be built over time with trust and respect.
